Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how ExpenseTracker collects, uses, and protects your personal and financial data while helping you track and manage your income and expenditures.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our platform:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and account details.
  • Financial Data: Income details, expense records, budget data, and transaction history.
  • Usage Data: Browsing history, interactions with our website, and how you use the tracker.
  • Cookies and Tracking: Data collected through cookies to enhance your user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Tracking and categorizing your expenses and income.
  • Generating financial reports and summaries to help manage your budget.
  • Personalizing your experience and offering relevant insights into your spending habits.
  • Sending updates, reminders, and newsletters (if subscribed).
  • Ensuring security and preventing fraudulent activities.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal or financial data. However, we may share it with trusted third parties such as:

  • Service providers assisting in the operation of our platform.
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Third-party advertisers (only anonymized data to improve our services).

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement strong security measures to protect your personal and financial data. However, we cannot guarantee absolute security due to the nature of the internet.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal and financial data. To exercise your rights, please contact us with your request.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our website for your reference.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@expensetracker.com.
